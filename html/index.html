How does SmartCourier work?

SmartCourier analyzes earnings reports and location data from all of its members to help them earn more. As soon as you upload your first batch of location and earnings data, you'll have access to:

  1. Insights from earnings trends to help you pick the most profitable companies to work for, areas to work, and times to work.
  2. Insights from earnings data from every restaurant in our database to help you avoid the time-waters.
  3. Personal performance analysis comparing you to every other rider in our database to show where you can be more efficent.
  4. Future earnings predictions to help you plan your work around moneymakers like poor weather, holidays and promotional campaigns from Gig companies.
  5. All of our raw earnings and rider data, forever, for you to use however you want.
